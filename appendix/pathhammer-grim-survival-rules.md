# PathHammer - Grim Survival Rules

## Limitations

### Hit Points

- After 5th level, characters stop gaining Hit Points from level advancement.
- Feat-based and ability-based Hit Point gains still apply.

## Conditions

### Wounded

- Losing dying gives you wounded, or increases your wounded value.
- Wounded increases incoming dying severity.
- Treat Wounds is harder against wounded characters.
- Full rest in safe conditions can reduce wounded.

### Limping

- Limping reduces speed by 10 feet, to a minimum of 5 feet.
- A full rest and successful Treat Wounds remove it.
