# Combat

## Critical Specializations

The following critical specializations are changed. Changed text in **bold**.

**Axe**: Choose one creature adjacent to the initial target and within reach.
If its AC is lower than your attack roll result for the critical hit, you deal
damage to that creature equal to the result of the weapon damage die you rolled
(including extra dice for its striking rune, if any). This amount isn’t
doubled, and no bonuses or other additional dice apply to this damage.
**If there is no eligible secondary target or the weapon has been thrown, you
instead make a vicious deep slash and deal 1 additional damage per weapon
damage die.**

> Out of all critical specializations, the axe is the only one that may not
> apply any effect if no additional target fulfills requirements or the weapon
> has been thrown. This change gives it a weaker version of the Pick
> specialization as a fallback option.
